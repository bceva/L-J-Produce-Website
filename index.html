About Us


L & J Produce company is a wholesale produce food distributor and owner of a food service warehouse.

We sell a wide range of quality fruits and vegetables to various fruit retailers and grocery stores around NYC.

We operate within the highest ethical standards and business practices. Our dedicated team, which we carefully select, continue to strive for ways to improve our service and meet the unique needs of our customers. Due to our strong relationships with the most high-quality growers in the industry, ranging from wholesale commodity growers, organic growers, and local family farmers, we provide the best products at affordable prices.
